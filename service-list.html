Our Services

We ensure you achieve the right look by offering a wide and comprehensive range of hair services that meet and exceed our client’s expectations and leave them feeling relaxed and rejuvenated. We handle all types of hair: – Caucasian Afro, Asian. We specialize in:-

  • Colors
  • Tints
  • Perms
  • Relaxers
  • Dreadlocks
  • Sets
  • Tongs
  • Flat iron
  • Braids
  • Hair Plaiting

A good hair cut is essential for looks and we therefore offer a menu that suits your needs by allowing you to update your look and make it work for your style.
For Gents we offer:-

  • Cuts for gents and boys
  • Beard cuts
For Ladies we offer:-
  • General cuts

  • Split end cuts

For Hand care we offer:-

  • Basic manicures
  • French manicure
  • Gel polishing
  • Polishing and Re-vanishing
  • Acrylics

For Foot care we offer: –

  • Basic pedicure
  • Paraffin wax pedicure
  • French pedicure
  • Foot massage
